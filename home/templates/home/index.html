We're Responsible

As an adventure store that sells outdoor clothing and hiking equipment, we pride ourselves in the responsible corporate partnerships we hold:

  • Recycled Packaging: We take our commitment to the environment seriously, even down to the packaging of our hiking clothes and equipment for camping in Ireland. Our innovative approach includes using biodegradable and recycled materials for packaging.
  • Reforest Nation: Join us as we lead the way in responsible corporate practices, planting trees with every sale to create a better, greener future. You can take on Ireland's toughest hike with peace of mind that you sourced your gear from a responsible company!

We Educate

We're thrilled to announce that we frequently host onsite and online courses, split into tent building and outdoor skills. Not only do we sell gear, but we aim to share expertise too. Explore key topics below:

  • Technical Skills and Safety: These are 2 foundational pillars upon which all our courses are built.
  • Leave no trace: We encourage our customers to enjoy and protect the outdoors responsibly, so we teach the core 'leave no trace' principles in all of our courses.

We're Green

See the four points below that show how we are socially responsible and giving back to the environment with every sale!

B Corporation

As a certified B Corporation, we maintain high standards of social and environmental performance, transparency and accountability across our business. This certification evaluates a company's overall social and environmental impact.

Ethical Sourcing

We are committed to ethically sourcing materials for our hiking clothes and equipment, ensuring fair labor practices and supporting local communities while delivering top-quality gear for your outdoor adventures.

Recycled Packaging

We're serious about the environment, even in packaging. Our innovative approach involves biodegradable and recycled materials, cutting waste and shrinking our shipping carbon footprint.

Tree Planting Partnership

For every purchase made on our platform, we partner with reforestation initiatives to plant a tree in areas affected by deforestation. Join us in our mission to not just sustain, but enhance our planet's natural beauty for generations to come.
