We're Responsible

As an adventure store that sells outdoor clothing and hiking equipment, we pride ourselves in the responsible corporate partnerships we hold:

  • Recycled Packaging: We take our commitment to the environment seriously, even down to the packaging of our hiking clothes and equipment for camping in Ireland. Our innovative approach includes using biodegradable and recycled materials for packaging, reducing waste and minimizing our carbon footprint throughout the shipping process.
  • Reforest Nation: Proudly partnering with Reforest Nation, we're not just a business, we're stewards of the outdoors. Join us as we lead the way in responsible corporate practices, planting trees with every sale to create a better, greener future. You can take on Ireland's toughest hike with peace of mind that you sourced your gear from a responsible company!

We're Green

See the four points below that show how we are socially responsible and giving back to the environment with every sale!

B Corporation

As a certified B Corporation, we uphold rigorous standards of social and environmental performance, transparency and accountability in every aspect of our business. This certificate measures a company's entire social and environmental impact.

Ethical Sourcing

We are committed to ethically sourcing materials for our hiking clothes and equipment, ensuring fair labor practices and supporting local communities while delivering top-quality gear for your outdoor adventures.

Recycled Packaging

We take our commitment to the environment seriously, even down to the packaging. Our innovative approach includes using biodegradable and recycled materials for packaging, reducing waste and minimizing our carbon footprint throughout the shipping process.

Tree Planting Partnership

For every purchase made on our platform, we partner with reforestation initiatives to plant a tree in areas affected by deforestation. Join us in our mission to not just sustain, but enhance our planet's natural beauty for generations to come.
